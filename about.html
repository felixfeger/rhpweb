About RHP

The Republic Highway Patrol was founded in 2022. The Republic Highway Patrol quickly grew, and took on the responsibilites of many state and federal agencies, which resulted in it's federalization in 2025.

With this new federalization, the Republic Highway Patrol is responsible for performing federal law enforcment functions such as securing federal buildings, and enforcing laws on all public highways.

RHP is made up of three bureaus, the traffic enforcement bureau, security bureau, and the investigative bureau. Alongside with RSCBP, RHP also performs some immigration functions such as carrying out lawful arrests.
