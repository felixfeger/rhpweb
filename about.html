About the Republic Highway Patrol

The Republic Highway Patrol (RHP) was founded in 2022 as the State of Republic's state police agency.

In 2025, following federal reforms, the agency was transformed into the official federal law enforcement organization of the Republic States of America.

RHP officers protect citizens, enforce federal laws, and maintain security across the nation's transportation network and federal facilities.

The agency is headquartered in Death Star City in Lego City County and operates stations across multiple counties.
